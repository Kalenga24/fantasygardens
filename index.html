Who We Are

Just Me Fantasy Gardens

Drawn in the heart of Lusaka, Just Me Fantasy Gardens was born out of a deep love for beautiful spaces, community, and the art of celebration. What began as a dream has grown into one of Lusaka's most sought-after event venues.

Our garden is more than a venue — it is a canvas. From the moment guests enter, they are transported into a world of lush greenery, carefully curated décor, and an atmosphere that simply cannot be replicated.

We take immense pride in honouring Zambian culture and traditions. Whether it's an authentic Chilanga Mulilo, a kitchen party full of song and laughter, or a bride walking down a garden aisle — we approach every event with passion and genuine care.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 4–8 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and peak seasons. However, we welcome last-minute enquiries and will always try to accommodate you.
Is a deposit required to secure my date? +
Yes, a 30% deposit is required to confirm and secure your event date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before your event.
Can I bring my own caterer? +
Absolutely. You are welcome to bring your own caterer or use our partner catering services. We have a dedicated kitchen preparation area available.
What happens if it rains? +
We have sheltered areas and can arrange tent/gazebo structures upon request. We always have a rain contingency plan ready so your event is never disrupted.
How many guests can the garden accommodate? +
Our garden can comfortably accommodate events from intimate gatherings of 20 people up to large celebrations of 500+ guests depending on the setup.
